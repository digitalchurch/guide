# Add Contact Information to your Footer

This guide will teach you how to add contact information to your website footer. This guide assumes that you are logged in to your site as an administrator.

1. From the front end of your site, hover over the settings button in the admin toolbar and click on Global Options. 
2. Click on Footer and then add a widget area to the footer.
3. Click on the widget area settings button and then click on the + Add a Widget button.
4. Click on the Text widget to add a text widget to the widget area.
5. Add your contact information and click publish.
