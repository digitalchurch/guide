# Adjust content for responsive screen widths

Our page builder uses four breakpoints for content that you can use to change the design and layout of your pages based on small, medium, large, and extra large screen widths. By default, we set our breakpoints at 480px and 960px. Any screens below 480px are considered small screens, 480px-960px are medium screens, and 960px and up are large screens. Most of our layouts set the content max width at 1440px as well, so on screens wider than 1440px, the content will remain at 1440px while the backgrounds may stretch from edge to edge.
