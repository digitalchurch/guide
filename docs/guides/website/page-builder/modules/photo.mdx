# Add a Photo Module to your page

## Launch the Page Builder

import Tabs from '@theme/Tabs';
import TabItem from '@theme/TabItem';

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="dashboard" label="Dashboard" default>
    On the pages view in the dashboard, hover over the page you would like to edit and click <b>Page Builder</b>.
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="toolbar" label="Toolbar">
    While viewing the page you would like to edit, click on the <b>Page Builder</b> button.
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="edit" label="Page Editor">
    From the dashboard page editor, click on the <b>Launch Page Builder</b> button.
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Drag a Photo module into the Page Content

In the top right corner, click on the + icon to add a new module. In the module tab, you'll find a Photo module. Click and drag it into the page content where you would like it to be placed.

## Set the Image Source

In the photo module, you will be able to choose an image from your Media Library or Upload a new image to your Media Library.

## Style your Photo

In the style tab, you'll find options for border radius, shadows, borders, etc.

## Advanced Tab

In the advanced tab, you'll find options for margins, custom classes & ID and import/export of settings or styles.

## Save and Publish

Once you are done editing your page, click on the <b>Done</b> button in the top right corner. If you are ready to publish your changes, click on the <b>Publish</b> button.
