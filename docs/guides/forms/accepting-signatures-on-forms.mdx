Sometimes you need to be able to accept a signature on a form. Here's how.

### 1. Enable the Add-On

Go to Settings > Plugins and find the "Gravity Forms Signatures Add-On" plugin.

### 2. Add the signature field to your form.

Edit your form and drag the signature field into the form where you need it.
